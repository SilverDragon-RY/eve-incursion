TLA incursions < Mixed Tank >
Marauder-Focused Community. Fast, efficient, elite.

Fleet Setup: 2 Nestor 2 Loki + 1 Vindi(ddd) + Marauder

Insight: up-time focused, have the best isk/hr, mixed tank means you don't need to worry about which ship you have trained into. You need to be at least a marauder to join, and that's not finished, after 24-hrs in fleet you will be tracked to upgrade into the DPS requirement. However if you met certain DPS you are guaranteed 2 spots when available (your alt will have higher priority then people on waitlist). Effort will pay off!

Contingency < Armor Tank >
Reborned from the old TOP, now the best beginner-friendly community.

Fleet Setup: 3 Nestor + Oneiros + Limited Starters + Main Force (Marauder, Vindi, Nightmare)

Currently the best community for beginners to join and build experience and stash. They have patient FC, walk you through your first fleet, tagging targets for each sites, and designed scheme for both your character, your ships and your mind. No matter which kind of specialist you wanna train into, there's sufficient help around.

The eldest community among all. They have experienced fc to point out your mistakes and save you from death, also tagging every sites so you know what to shoot. They also fleet regularly, is a good place to make isk and gain experience.
